About

The Dream Sandwich is named after Kalpana,the creator of the product.It was launched as time-pass business by Kalpana. She always had the passion to cook.Her family loved the food and varieties she made! So just to quench her thirst to cook Kalpana started to make sandwiches in the evenings and sell them to the nearby stores.To her surprise the business took off easily just by word from one store to another. Today it is HUGE!!!

The Varieties

The Veg Delight

The tomatoes, cucumbers, potatoes and other ingredient vegetables are cut into thin slices and stacked between two slices of bread (of your choice) that have been spread with butter and spicy green chutney on the inner sides.

The FootLong Sandwich

Quench your hunger with the lipsmacking taste of the footlong sandwich.It comes with a range of fillings, sprinkled with spices and onions.Choose a filling of your choice and lose yourself in the delight.

The Club Sandwich

Filled with the best of vegetables and sauces, it has a classified taste. The dream special fruit variety has a mix of seasonal fruit filling into the club, giving it has all the goodness of health and taste.Served best with chips and drinks.
